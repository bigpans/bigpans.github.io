Kaze and the Wild Masks

Inspired by the greatest hits of the 90's, Kaze and the Wild Masks is a platformer tailored to reproduce the enjoyable experience of playing the classics. Chain bounce off of enemy heads, explore a variety of challenges and uncover secrets feeling like you never left your mom's house.

  • Platforms: Windows | Mac | PS4 | Xbox One
  • Steam Page: Steam (add to wishlist!)
  • Official Website: PlayKaze

Sugar World

In Sugar World, you are a grain of sugar that needs to fulfill its purpose of reaching the coffee.

  • Platform: Windows/WebGL
  • Play: Web - Itchio
  • Download: Windows
  • Ludum Dare Homepage: Click here

Namcap's Nightmare

Namcap's Nightmare is a 3D First-person horror game. It was made with my friend @kaduwall for the Ludum Dare 37 competition. I was responsible for all coding and scripting in this project.

  • Platform: Windows/Mac/Linux
  • Download: LD Homepage

FireBrawl

FireBrawl is a Fast-paced 2D two-button mashing game, inspired by some mini-games from N64 Pokemon. This game was made for the Ludum Dare 36 competition. I was responsible for all coding and animation of this game.

  • Platform: Windows
  • Download: Itch.io
  • Other: LD Homepage

SharkDiver

SharkDiver is a casual game made for the Ludum Dare competition 35. I was responsible for all assets, coding and animation in this game.

  • Platform: Android/iOS/Windows/HTML5
  • Play: WebGL
  • Download: LD Homepage
  • Play Store: Android
